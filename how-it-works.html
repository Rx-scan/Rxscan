How It Works

Verify any medicine in Nigeria using our simple 4-step process

1

Download & Open the App

Get the RxScan app from Google Play Store or Apple App Store. Launch it and tap the "Verify Drug" button on the homepage.

2

Find the NAFDAC Number

Look for the NAFDAC registration number on your medicine pack. It usually starts with "NAFDAC REG NO" followed by numbers and letters.

3

Enter & Verify

Type the NAFDAC number exactly as it appears on the pack. Tap "Verify" and wait for the instant results.

4

Take Safe Action

Based on the results, either use your safe medicine or report suspicious drugs. We make it easy to take the right action.
