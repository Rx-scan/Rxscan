Report Fake Drug

Help protect others by reporting suspected fake or counterfeit medicines

Important Safety Notice

If you suspect you have a fake drug, DO NOT use it. Stop taking the medicine immediately and consult with a healthcare professional for a safe alternative.

Please include photos of the packaging, pills/tablets, and any suspicious features

We may contact you for additional information to help with the investigation

What happens after you report?

  • Your report is sent directly to NAFDAC and relevant authorities
  • We investigate the pharmacy/seller and the drug batch
  • Other users are warned about potential fake drugs in that area
  • You help prevent others from buying the same fake medicine
  • We may contact you for additional information if needed
